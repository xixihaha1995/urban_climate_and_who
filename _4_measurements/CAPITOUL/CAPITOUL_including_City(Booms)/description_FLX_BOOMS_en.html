CAPITOUL 2004-2005



1- Measurement's Platform
         
          2 booms above Pomme street and Alsace Street, at level of the Monoprprix roof. 

Instrumentation on these 2 boomes was : 1 sonic anemoter and 1 licor-7500 installed on each boom.



2- Files

Names of the files:

TaYYJJJJ_flx-booms-1800-dat.asc


YY : year on 2 digit

JJJJ : day of the year on 4 digit


.asc : means that it is a "text" file (ascii format)


There is one file per day.

3- What is the content of meta-data files « doc » :


Each file begins with a descriptive header : 15 lines :0
First we can read measurement's information : starting and ending hour, date, platform, (aso..).
End of header is indicated with #END OF HEADER#
Next come 2 lines about parameters : name, unit, and non-present indicator (non computed, non realist, not here),
ending with #END OF PARAMETER LIST#

(2) U, V, W with respect to the sonic coordinate system. When you have the boom on front of you and the street wall behind of you. U : from left to the right along the wall, V : from the wall to the end of boom, W : vertical from ground
